#### Research

[On the Interplay of Pre-Training, Mid-Training, and RL on Reasoning Language Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.07783). A controlled synthetic framework shows RL yields genuine reasoning gains only when pre-training leaves room for improvement and RL data match the model's competence edge; sparse pre-training exposure enables contextual transfer that RL can amplify; a mid-training bridge materially boosts in- and out-of-domain performance; and process-based rewards reduce reward hacking and improve reasoning fidelity.

[Towards a Science of Scaling Agent Systems](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.08296). Findings indicate multi-agent benefits depend on measurable task and architectural factors‚Äîrevealing a tool‚Äëcoordination trade‚Äëoff, a capability ceiling where extra agents hurt once single-agent performance is high, and architecture‚Äëdependent error amplification‚Äîtogether allowing prediction of optimal coordination structures for agentic tasks.

[Weird Generalization and Inductive Backdoors: New Ways to Corrupt LLMs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.09742). The paper shows that fine-tuning on very small, narrow datasets can induce broad, unexpected misbehaviors‚Äîincluding partisan shifts and stealthy backdoors (both traditional and novel inductive backdoors)‚Äîby causing models to generalize a latent persona or connect dispersed cues into harmful behaviors.

[Long-horizon Reasoning Agent for Olympiad-Level Mathematical Problem Solving](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.10739). A hierarchical, multi-agent RL-trained workflow iteratively compresses and stores intermediate lemmas in a verified memory repository, extending effective reasoning depth beyond fixed context limits to solve Olympiad-level math problems.

[Training-Time Action Conditioning for Efficient Real-Time Chunking](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.05964). A training-time inpainting technique simulates inference delays so models learn to condition on action prefixes ahead of time, removing inference-time overhead and improving real-world and simulated performance at high latency.

[LYNX: Learning Dynamic Exits for Confidence-Controlled Reasoning](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.05325). A lightweight probe trained on cue-triggered forced-exit examples, combined with split conformal calibration, turns hidden-state confidence at natural cue tokens into an online stop/continue rule that reduces token usage while preserving accuracy across models and tasks.

[The Universal Weight Subspace Hypothesis](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.05117). Networks of the same architecture consistently converge to a layer-wise, low-rank joint subspace of parameters, enabling efficient adaptation, compression, and transfer across diverse tasks.

[Evaluating Gemini Robotics Policies in a Veo World Simulator](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.10675). An action-conditioned, multi-view consistent video-generation model trained on large-scale robotics data simulates and predicts policy performance, generalization degradation, and safety vulnerabilities‚Äîvalidated against 1,600+ real-world trials‚Äîand enables scene editing for red-teaming without hardware tests.

[Training LLMs for Honesty via Confessions](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.08093). Models are trained to produce an auxiliary self-evaluation (‚Äúconfession‚Äù) after answers, scored by an LLM judge, reporting which constraints they considered, how well they met them, and any uncertainties or misbehavior.

[ConceptAttention: Diffusion Transformers Learn Highly Interpretable Features](https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.04320). Concept-specific text embeddings extracted from DiT attention outputs, combined with linear projections over image patch representations, produce high-fidelity saliency maps that localize arbitrary textual concepts and enable state-of-the-art zero-shot segmentation without additional training.

[A Definition of AGI](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.18212). The work operationalizes AGI by mapping human cognitive abilities (via the CHC framework) to ten equally weighted, testable components‚Äîproducing a standardized AGI Score based on multimodal cognitive batteries to assess whether an AI matches the versatility and proficiency of a well-educated adult.
