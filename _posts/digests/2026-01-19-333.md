#### [Sequoia to invest in Anthropic, breaking VC taboo on backing rivals: FT](https://techcrunch.com/2026/01/18/sequoia-to-invest-in-anthropic-breaking-vc-taboo-on-backing-rivals-ft/)

#### [Zhipu AI breaks US chip reliance with first major model trained on Huawei stack](https://www.scmp.com/tech/tech-war/article/3339869/zhipu-ai-breaks-us-chip-reliance-first-major-model-trained-huawei-stack)
![](https://cdn.i-scmp.com/sites/default/files/styles/og_image_scmp_generic/public/d8/images/canvas/2026/01/14/323c47dc-0d7f-4c26-8223-6cbbbce1ac07_550521d0.jpg?itok=JVa7Ka0z&v=1768381476)

Zhipu AI announced that its new open-source image generation model, GLM-Image, was trained entirely on a domestic Huawei stack‚Äîmarking a first for a ‚Äúpowerful‚Äù model developed without US chips. The design echoes approaches like Google DeepMind‚Äôs Nano Banana Pro, jointly generating high-fidelity images and coherent text. By labeling the model ‚Äúpowerful‚Äù and open-source, Zhipu signals both performance ambitions and community adoption potential, while emphasizing the concrete stack choices‚ÄîAscend 800T A2 + MindSpore‚Äîand the end-to-end locally sourced training pipeline as the core achievement.

This benchmark evaluates 32 real-world scenarios comprising 138 long-horizon tasks (averaging ~1M tokens and ~90 tool calls each) to measure agents‚Äô multi-turn, tool-using, and context-maintenance capabilities using an automated user-simulation and Docker sandbox pipeline.

[Reasoning Models Generate Societies of Thought](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.10825). The authors report improved problem-solving by producing multiple interacting agent-like reasoning streams that provide diverse perspectives and organized collaboration.

[The Assistant Axis: Situating and Stabilizing the Default Persona of Language Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.10387). The work identifies a dominant ‚ÄúAssistant Axis‚Äù in model activations that locates the default assistant persona, shows that shifts along this axis predict and enable persona drift during conversations, and demonstrates an activation-clamping method that reduces harmful or off-persona responses without hurting capability.

[Molmo2: Open Weights and Data for Vision-Language Models with Video Understanding and Grounding](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.10611). [Memory Bank Compression for Continual Adaptation of Large Language Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.00756). A method compresses and optimizes external memory banks with learned codebooks so LLMs can continually adapt to new data while preserving previously learned performance.

[SeedFold: Scaling Biomolecular Structure Prediction](https://arxiv.org/html/2512.24354v1). This ByteDance-led project outlines methods and contributions for scaling biomolecular structure prediction across larger datasets and collaborator teams.
